About the Body

The body

remembers.

The body speaks softly before it begins to ache. In tension, in breath, in the quiet exhaustion we learn to ignore. At Sukha Vihara, we believe wellness is not correction, but a gentle return — to poise, softness, and the wisdom already held within the body. Through slow rituals and intentional touch, we create moments that invite you to pause, release, and listen again.
